Adam Maichrzik
Cube

This art I wanted to paint perfectly. Straight lines, pure black color, minimalistic style. I was able to do it only because of use of the painting tape. I wasn't able to make it PERFECT by nature, by just holding the brush in my hand and painting. And that is the main thought. We want to achieve the level of perfection but we are not able to do it without the "painting tape" that is correcting our imperfections.

Nobody is perfect.

This is the most common to see on social medias, for example Instagram.
We want to make ourself perfect because we se "perfection" on others, but we dont think and we dont notice the "painting tape" - the amount of corrections made by apps and programs for editing the photos. This is not pure from nature perfectness, this is fake perfectness achieved by using programs.
